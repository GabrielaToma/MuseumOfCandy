MUSEUM OF CANDY

Welcome to our precious Museum of Candy!
Born to offer a world full of magical tastes, a world that represents a childhood dream come true.
Enter through our gates, leave your adult life behind and let yourself be blown away.

ABOUT

Museum of Candy opened its gates to the world in 2018.
It represents our wish to not let adulthood steal some of the happiness we had as children. With the diet culture becoming more and more popular, some of our childhood joys became the enemy of the modern world.
Let us offer you a day where calories aren't counted and sugar isn't demonized. A day of sweetness, history and memories.
